About the Course

Generative AI for Work & Research is an intensive, guided 8‑week course designed to help researchers and professionals responsibly integrate generative AI into everyday workflows. The program emphasizes no‑code tools, ethical automation, and transparent AI‑assisted outputs.

  • Websites, Documents & Presentations: Understanding LLMs and generating structured research outputs.
  • AI‑Automated Data Analysis: Using AI tools to process, visualize, and report data responsibly.
  • GenAI for Research & Text Analysis: Literature reviews, summarization, and knowledge synthesis.
  • Custom GPTs & Chatbots: Building task‑specific assistants and RAG‑based systems.
  • AI for Task Automation: Designing ethical automation workflows and agents.
  • GenAI for Communication & Outreach: Creating clear, accurate research communication assets.
  • Capstone Project: Developing and presenting a final AI‑driven solution.
